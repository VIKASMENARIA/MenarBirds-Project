About Menarwings

A Community-Led Conservation Story

Located just 45km from Udaipur, Menar is more than just a village; it's a living example of coexistence. Known as the "Bird Village," it is a proposed Ramsar site managed entirely by the local community.

Every winter, the Brahma and Dhand ponds come alive with the calls of thousands of migratory birds. From the majestic Great Crested Grebe (locally known as Shiva Dubdubi) to the vibrant Flamingos, Menar is a theatre of nature.
